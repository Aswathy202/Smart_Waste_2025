About SmartWaste

Smart Waste Management & Recycling Tracker for a Sustainable Future ♻️

Our Mission

SmartWaste is a digital waste management and recycling platform that helps cities, communities, and individuals efficiently manage waste collection and recycling. Our mission is to make waste management smarter, cleaner, and more transparent by connecting citizens, collectors, and administrators through a single system.

With SmartWaste, we aim to promote sustainability, reduce landfill waste, and encourage recycling culture through technology and community collaboration.

Why Choose SmartWaste?

Eco-Friendly Approach

Encourages recycling, waste segregation, and cleaner urban spaces.

Technology-Driven

Built using Django, Bootstrap, and interactive data visualization tools.

Community Focused

Brings citizens, collectors, and administrators together to build greener cities.

Our Vision

To create a digital ecosystem that simplifies waste management, empowers citizens, and fosters accountability in every level of the recycling chain — making smart cities greener and more efficient.
